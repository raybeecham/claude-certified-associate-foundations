## Certification source discipline

Use the official preparation course and current candidate materials for:

- exam registration;
- availability;
- delivery method;
- time limit;
- number and style of questions;
- identity requirements;
- rescheduling rules;
- score and retake policies; and
- the current official domain weighting.

This repository deliberately avoids asserting those volatile details without a current public candidate guide.
