Mental models explain how to think about AI systems before selecting features.

## Available models

1. [Workflow Over Prompts](workflow-over-prompts.md)
2. [Deterministic vs. Generative](deterministic-vs-generative.md)
3. [Context as Working Memory](context-as-working-memory.md)

## Use

Return to these when a workflow becomes confusing or over-engineered.

Ask:

- Are we solving the business problem or polishing prompts?
- Is this task appropriate for probabilistic generation?
- Which facts and rules must remain deterministic?
- Is the active context focused and authoritative?
- Who is accountable for the result?
