## Required structure

Every case study should document:

1. business problem;
2. desired outcome and audience;
3. evidence and authorization;
4. workspace selection;
5. capability selection;
6. model-selection hypothesis;
7. context strategy;
8. workflow architecture;
9. prompt contract;
10. evaluation plan;
11. governance and human review;
12. results;
13. failure analysis; and
14. lessons learned.

Use fictional, synthetic, public, or explicitly authorized information only.
