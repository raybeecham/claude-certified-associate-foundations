# Roadmap

## Version 0.1

- Seven complete study domains
- Original quizzes and flashcards
- Two balanced practice exams
- Hands-on labs and security-oriented case studies
- Offline study CLI
- Content validation and CI

## Version 0.2

- Additional scenario bank
- Tagged difficulty and objective metadata
- JSON export for common flashcard applications
- Expanded model-selection and tool-use labs
- Accessibility review

## Version 0.3

- Optional local web dashboard
- Trend reporting from local study history
- Community-contributed case studies
- Automated official-link health checks

## Non-goals

- Exam dumps
- Claims of official endorsement
- Memorization of temporary model pricing or limits
- Automated collection of private course content
